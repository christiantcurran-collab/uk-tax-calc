Important Considerations

Past Performance

Historical data doesn't guarantee future results. Property markets can be affected by economic conditions, interest rates, and local factors.

Location Matters

This projection uses UK-wide averages. Actual growth varies significantly by region, city, and neighborhood.

Property Condition

Maintenance, improvements, and property condition significantly impact value. This projection assumes average upkeep.

Costs Not Included

This doesn't account for mortgage interest, maintenance costs, insurance, or transaction fees when buying/selling.
