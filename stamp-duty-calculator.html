About Stamp Duty

Stamp Duty Land Tax (SDLT) applies in England, Wales, and Northern Ireland. Land and Buildings Transaction Tax (LBTT) applies in Scotland. Both are taxes paid when you buy a property.

First-Time Buyers

UK: No SDLT up to £300,000, then 5% up to £500,000.
Scotland: Nil rate band increased to £175,000 (saving up to £600).

Additional Properties

UK: Extra 5% surcharge on all bands if you own another property.
Scotland: Extra 6% on all bands (Additional Dwelling Supplement).

When to Pay

You must pay stamp duty within 14 days of completion. Your solicitor usually handles this as part of the purchase process.

Replacing Main Residence

You won't pay the additional property surcharge if you sell your previous main home within 36 months of buying the new one.
