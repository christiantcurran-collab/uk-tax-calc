What to Look For in a Savings Account

  • Interest Rate (AER) - The Annual Equivalent Rate shows what you'll earn over a year
  • Access - Easy access, notice periods, or fixed terms
  • FSCS Protection - Ensure your savings are protected up to £85,000
  • Minimum Balance - Some accounts require minimum deposits
  • Withdrawal Limits - Check for any restrictions on accessing your money

Types of Savings Accounts

Easy Access

Withdraw money anytime without penalties. Lower rates but maximum flexibility.

Notice Accounts

Give notice (30-120 days) before withdrawing. Higher rates than easy access.

Regular Savers

Save a fixed amount monthly. Often offer the highest rates but with restrictions.

Fixed Rate Bonds

Lock in your rate for guaranteed returns
