# PropellerAds Banner Setup Instructions

## Step 1: Create Banner Ad Zones

1. Go to your PropellerAds dashboard: https://partners.propellerads.com/
2. Click on **"Websites & Apps"** → **"Add New Zone"**
3. Create 3 banner zones:

### Top Banner (728x90)
- **Zone Type**: Banner
- **Size**: 728x90 (Leaderboard)
- **Name**: UK Tax Calc - Top Banner
- Copy the zone key/ID

### Sidebar Banner (300x250)
- **Zone Type**: Banner
- **Size**: 300x250 (Medium Rectangle)
- **Name**: UK Tax Calc - Sidebar
- Copy the zone key/ID

### Bottom Banner (728x90)
- **Zone Type**: Banner
- **Size**: 728x90 (Leaderboard)
- **Name**: UK Tax Calc - Bottom Banner
- Copy the zone key/ID

## Step 2: Update index-propeller.html

Replace these placeholders with your actual zone keys:

1. **Line 22**: Replace `YOUR_TOP_BANNER_KEY` with your top banner key
2. **Line 28**: Replace `YOUR_TOP_BANNER_KEY` with your top banner key
3. **Line 221-226**: Replace `YOUR_SIDEBAR_BANNER_KEY` (both lines)
4. **Line 391-396**: Replace `YOUR_BOTTOM_BANNER_KEY` (both lines)

## Step 3: Deploy

Once you have your keys:
1. Rename `index-propeller.html` to `index.html`
2. Commit and push to `uk-tax-calc` repository
3. Deploy to Render
