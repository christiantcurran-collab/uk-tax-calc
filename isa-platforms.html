Key Points

  • Annual fees are charged as a percentage of your portfolio value or as a flat monthly fee
  • Dealing fees apply each time you buy or sell investments
  • Flat fee platforms (like Interactive Investor) are better value for larger portfolios
  • Percentage fee platforms are usually cheaper for smaller portfolios
  • Commission-free platforms (Trading 212, InvestEngine) are ideal for beginners or small amounts
  • Consider fund selection - not all platforms offer all investments
  • Check if you need phone support or if online/app is sufficient
