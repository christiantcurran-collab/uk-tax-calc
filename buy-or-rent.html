Factors to Consider

Property Growth

Historical UK average is around 4% annually, but this varies significantly by location and time period. Property values can fall.

Maintenance & Repairs

As a homeowner, you're responsible for all maintenance and repairs. Budget around 1% of property value annually.

Flexibility

Renting offers more flexibility to move. Selling a property takes time and incurs estate agent fees (typically 1-3%).

Interest Rates

Mortgage rates can change when you remortgage. Fixed-rate periods typically last 2-5 years.

Disclaimer: This calculator provides estimates for comparison purposes only. It doesn't include all costs (e.g., maintenance, insurance, council tax). Property values can fall as well as rise. Always seek professional financial advice.
